Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked
Questions

What does pre-registration cost?

It is completely free. You can secure your founding member spot without any payment.

Is my business data secure?

Absolutely. We employ bank-grade encryption and follow international security standards. Your data is stored on secure servers and strict access controls.

What payment methods are supported?

We will integrate with major Pakistani banks, JazzCash, Easypaisa, and Raast. We will also support Credit/Debit Cards, 1Link, Sadapay, Nayapay, and also cash payments for offline transactions.

When will you launch?

We launch once we reach atleast 50 pre-registrations and development is complete. You will be notified via SMS or phone call with early access to the platform.

How does the E-Mandate system work?

E-Mandates allow automatic recurring deductions from your customer's bank account. Once authorized, payments are collected automatically on scheduled dates without requiring manual intervention.

Can I import my existing customer list?

Yes! You can easily import your existing customers via CSV upload. Our onboarding team will help you migrate your data seamlessly.

What happens if a payment fails?

Our Smart Dunning engine automatically retries failed payments and sends reminders via WhatsApp, SMS, and push notifications. You can customize the retry schedule and messaging.

Is there a transaction fee?

We charge a small monthly fee on successful collections. As a founding member, you'll enjoy 10% off standard rates for the first year. Detailed pricing will be shared during onboarding.
